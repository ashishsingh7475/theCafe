ABOUT THE CAFE

Welcome to The Café, the soul of Guwahati’s coffee scene and your haven for unforgettable moments! Tucked away in the city’s lively streets, this cozy yet modern spot is where flavors blend seamlessly with stories. Whether you’re a coffee enthusiast, tea lover, or dessert aficionado, The Café is your ultimate destination for a sensory treat. Our menu is a delightful mix of global café staples and local Assamese favorites. From freshly brewed artisanal coffees to fragrant teas and delectable desserts, there’s something to suit every palate. Be sure to try our signature "Assamese Masala Latte", a bold twist on tradition, or the crowd-favorite "Smoked Chicken Pita Pockets", guaranteed to leave you craving more! Step into The Café’s inviting ambiance, with warm wooden interiors, soft lighting, and lush greenery—perfect for a quiet moment of introspection or a lively catch-up session with friends. Our live acoustic evenings and art showcases make it more than just a café—it’s a vibrant community hub where creativity and connection thrive. At The Café, every cup tells a story, and every visit becomes a cherished memory. Come for the flavors, stay for the vibe, and discover why life tastes better at The Café!

In addition to our full espresso and brew bar menu, we serve fresh made-to-order breakfast and lunch sandwiches, as well as a selection of sides and salads and other good stuff.

"Use products from nature for what it's worth - but never too early, nor too late." Fresh is the new sweet.

Chef, Coffeeist and Owner: Ashish Singh

Opening hours: everyday from 6am to 5pm.
